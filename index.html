About

About COMSA

COMSA is a students association of the CSE department formed to develop scholastic and technical approach among the students.

About our department

Computer Science Engineering (CSE) encompasses a variety of topics that relate to computation, like analysis of algorithms, programming languages, program design, software, and computer hardware with labs possessing state of the art equipment. Computer Science engineering has roots in electrical engineering, mathematics, and linguistics.

VED NANDE President

To the batch of 2023,

“I never dream of success; I worked for it” - Easter Lauder

Dear students, you have surely held our heads high with your results and achievements. In spite of this being a crushing year for all of us you didn’t fail to make us proud. What extraordinary these 4 years have been. Completing these challenging college years during these trying times of COVID-19 pandemic is remarkable since it has been inevitable to face and accept the new normal landscape of education.

You stayed focused in the coursework and contributed to the college community by engagement in the clubs and activities whether in-person or online. The pandemic didn’t stop you from what you wanted to achieve. I know I was very strict to you at times but that was for your own betterment. I want my students to shine in whichever field they step in. I will be missing you all and feeling very warm while writing this message for you. You all are very dear to me and I wish you all to be connected to this department as it is yours.

I hope you'll come back and tell us your success stories, visit campus and share your hard-won wisdom with the ones yet to start here. If you made it through this year, you can make it through another day. Onwards and upwards! Best wishes to you in all of your future endeavours !

Dr. Latesh Bhagat
